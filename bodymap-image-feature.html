Description

The VR feature was designed to seamlessly integrate medical content with 3D human models, facilitating the transition of medical students from academic courses to cadaver laboratories.

Overview

Medical Augmented Intelligence (MAI) is a software startup dedicated to immersive, collaborative VR teaching and learning.

BodyMap, our VR human anatomy app, assists medical students in understanding the location of anatomical structures through 3D VR visualization.
