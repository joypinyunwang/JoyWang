The Accompanying Growth Furniture

Description

The Accompanying Growth Furniture is an aesthetically pleasing series designed to accompany children as they grow.

Project Type

Furniture design

Team

Independent project;
Graduate project

Awards‍

  1. Nominated in the 2021 Taiwan International Student Design Competition.
  2. Nominated for the 2021 Vision Get Wild Award.

Duration

2 months (Oct 2020 - Dec 2020)

Tools

Sketching, Creo

Overview

This furniture is ideal for family spaces, providing a comfortable resting area for all members. The Accompanying Growth Furniture features a dual-purpose table and chair that can be adjusted in height, catering to the evolving needs of children's growth and adapting to various usage scenarios as they progress through different stages of development.

Problem Space

Understanding

Commonly, tables and chairs in the market feature adjustable tabletop heights, whether through manual or electric mechanisms.

The core concept involves various methods of height adjustment, and the design innovation focuses on the folding functionality incorporated into the legs of both the table and chair.

How to use

At a lower height, it caters to preschool children, offering horizontal storage for toys and art supplies, encouraging parent-child interaction. At a higher setting, it suits children and parents beyond the preschool age. The vertical storage space is designed for books or daily necessities.
