Personal Carbon Recording App

Description

Personal App designed for Taipei citizens to track and monitor transportation-related carbon emissions.

Project Type

Personal side project; Design for social impact

Team

Individual

My role

I played a core part in user interviews, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

Duration

8 weeks in total (Aug-Oct 2023)

Tools

Figma, Notion

Overview

I aimed to explore significant environmental issues in Taiwan, particularly the challenge of carbon emissions. Visualizing these emissions is challenging, hindering effective understanding of environmental improvements.

My goal is to raise public awareness, specifically focusing on transportation-related carbon emissions.

Problem Space

Problem Statement

How can we help users empathize with their daily carbon footprint and encourage actions to reduce emissions?

User Research

I conducted user interviews with 5 individuals to gain diverse insights swiftly.
The research goals in this phase are:

  1. Understanding the general public's awareness of carbon emission issues.
  2. Identifying the functions that users find most appealing among the initially conceived features.
  3. Recognizing common user behaviors related to the tasks my product aims to address.
