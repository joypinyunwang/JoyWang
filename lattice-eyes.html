Lattice Eyes

Description

This project exemplifies the application of iconic design principles to the field of lighting product design.

Project Type

Industrial design

Team

Independent project,
School project

Overview

The beauty of the window frame lies in its capacity to blend natural light and shadow with artificial space, drawing inspiration from traditional Chinese aesthetics and the harmonious integration of nature and humanity. I use the elements of the window frame to showcase the interplay of light, shadow, and reality.

Duration

2 months (Oct 2019 - Dec 2019)

Tools

Sketching, Keyshot

Concept

Just as with negative film developed in the darkroom, my focus is often on the result after processing rather than the inherent characteristics of the original object. At first glance, it may appear to be an ordinary chandelier; however, when the light source is activated, a decorative pattern, reminiscent of a mullion graphic, emerges on the ground.

Experiment Model Testing

Projection testing

Varied patterns emerge based on light and lamp distance.
